District Boundaries & Public Housing Estates

Coverage: 22°08' — 22°35' N  |  113°49' — 114°31' E

Central & Western District (中西區)

Boundary:

  • West: From Victoria Road / Mount Davis to Kennedy Town waterfront
  • East: Along Canal Road Flyover / Arsenal Street boundary with Wan Chai District
  • North: Victoria Harbour waterfront (from Kennedy Town Praya to Admiralty)
  • South: Along The Peak / Hatton Road / Pok Fu Lam Road boundary with Southern District

Key Areas:

Kennedy Town, Sai Wan, Shek Tong Tsui, Sai Ying Pun, Sheung Wan, Central, Admiralty (west), Mid-Levels, The Peak

Public Housing Estates:

  • Sai Wan Estate (西環邨) — Kennedy Town
  • Kwun Lung Lau (觀龍樓) — Kennedy Town

Southern District (南區)

Boundary:

  • North: Along Pok Fu Lam Road / The Peak / Wong Nai Chung Gap / Tai Tam Road boundary with Central & Western and Wan Chai Districts
  • East: From Tai Tam to Shek O, including D'Aguilar Peninsula
  • South: South China Sea coastline
  • West: Pok Fu Lam / Victoria Road boundary with Central & Western District

Key Areas:

Pok Fu Lam, Aberdeen, Ap Lei Chau, Wong Chuk Hang, Shouson Hill, Deep Water Bay, Repulse Bay, Stanley, Tai Tam, Shek O, Chung Hom Kok

Public Housing Estates:

  • Wah Fu (I) Estate (華富(一)邨) — Pok Fu Lam
  • Wah Fu (II) Estate (華富(二)邨) — Pok Fu Lam
  • Wah Kwai Estate (華貴邨) — Pok Fu Lam
  • Shek Pai Wan Estate (石排灣邨) — Aberdeen
  • Tin Wan Estate (田灣邨) — Aberdeen
  • Yue Kwong Chuen (漁光村) — Aberdeen
  • Ap Lei Chau Estate (鴨脷洲邨) — Ap Lei Chau
  • Lei Tung Estate (利東邨) — Ap Lei Chau
